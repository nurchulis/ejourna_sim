JISKA (Jurnal Informatika Sunan Kalijaga)

JISKa (Jurnal Informatika Sunan Kalijaga) is a journal that tries to study and develop the concept of Integration and Interconnection of religion and Informatics which published by Department of Information Engineering UIN Sunan Kalijaga Yogyakarta. JISka provides a forum for lecturers, researchers, students and practitioners to publish original research articles, review articles from contributors, and new technologies related to informatics from various disciplines.
